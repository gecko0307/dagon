# Jolt Physics

[Jolt Physics](https://github.com/jrouwe/JoltPhysics) is a robust and performant Open Source real-time physics engine by Jorrit Rouwe of Guerrilla Games. Battle-tested in AAA production, Jolt is gaining popularity and is used by major free game engines such as Godot, NeoAxis, GDevelop, Gaijin Entertainment's Dagor Engine, and many others. Dagon includes Jolt as an alternative to Newton.
